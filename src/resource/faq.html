Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an account?

    To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the login page. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. After submitting, you'll receive a confirmation email with instructions to activate your account.

  • How can I reset my password?

    If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. For security reasons, the reset link is only valid for 24 hours.

  • How do I add a new transaction?

    To add a new transaction, go to the Transactions page and click the "Add Transaction" button. Fill in the required details such as amount, category, date, and type (income or expense). You can also add a description for better record-keeping.

  • Can I edit or delete a transaction?

    Yes, you can edit or delete any transaction you've created. On the Transactions page, find the transaction you want to modify, then click the edit (pencil) or delete (trash) icon. When editing, you can change any details of the transaction.

  • How do I create a savings goal?

    To create a savings goal, navigate to the Goals page and click "Add New Goal." Enter a title, target amount, and optional category. You can also set a target date to help you plan your savings schedule.

  • What happens when I delete a goal?

    When you delete a goal, any funds you've allocated to that goal will be returned to your account balance. A transaction record will be created showing this refund. Any recurring deposits set up for the goal will also be deleted.

  • How do I create a new category?

    To create a new category, go to the Categories page and click "Add Category." Enter a name for your category and select an emoji to represent it. You can also set a budget for the category to help track your spending.

  • How do category budgets work?

    Category budgets allow you to set spending limits for different categories. On the Categories page, click "Set Budget" and select a category and monthly budget amount. The system will track your spending in each category and show you how much of your budget you've used.

  • How do I set up recurring payments?

    To set up recurring payments, go to the Recurring Purchases page and click "Add Recurring Payment." Enter the payment details including amount, frequency (weekly, monthly, etc.), and description. You can also set up recurring deposits for your goals on the Goals page.

  • Can I edit or delete recurring payments?

    Yes, you can edit or delete recurring payments at any time. On the Recurring Purchases page, find the payment you want to modify and click the edit or delete icon. Changes will apply to future payments only.

  • How do I change my profile information?

    To update your profile information, go to the Account Settings page by clicking on your profile icon in the top-right corner. From there, you can edit your name, email address, and other personal details. Don't forget to click "Save Changes" when you're done.

  • Is my financial data secure?

    Absolutely! We take your security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. We never share your financial information with third parties without your explicit consent.

    Additionally, we implement strict access controls and regular security audits to ensure your data remains protected at all times.

  • Can I export my financial data?

    Yes, you can export your financial data at any time. Go to the Summaries page and click on the "Export Data" button. You can choose to export in various formats including CSV, PDF, or Excel. This feature is useful for tax preparation or if you want to analyze your data in external tools.

  • How do I filter transactions?

    On the Transactions page, you'll find filter options at the top of the transaction list. You can filter by date range, category, transaction type (income or expense), and amount range. You can also use the search box to find specific transactions by description or other details.

  • Can I import transactions from my bank?

    Not yet, but we're working on it! In an upcoming update, we'll introduce the ability to securely connect to your bank accounts and automatically import transactions. This feature will save you time and ensure your financial data is always up to date.

    In the meantime, you can manually add transactions or import them using our CSV template.

  • How are recurring deposits to my goals processed?

    When you set up a recurring deposit for a goal, the system automatically transfers the specified amount from your balance to your goal at the scheduled interval (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.). Each deposit creates a transaction record and updates your goal's progress.

    You can view all scheduled deposits in the Recurring Payments section of your Dashboard.

  • What happens when I reach my goal target?

    Congratulations! When you reach your goal target, you'll receive a notification celebrating your achievement. The goal will be marked as completed, and you'll have several options:

    1. Keep the goal active and continue adding funds (for example, if it's an ongoing savings goal).

    2. Create a new goal with the accumulated funds as a starting point.

    3. Return the funds to your main balance by deleting the goal.

    Any recurring deposits associated with the goal will continue unless you manually cancel them.

  • What are the default categories and can I customize them?

    Vaultly comes with several default categories like Food, Transportation, Housing, Entertainment, etc. You can definitely customize these!

    You can add your own custom categories with personalized names and emoji icons. On the Categories page, click "Add Category" and enter the details. You can also set budgets for both default and custom categories.

  • How do I track spending in a specific category over time?

    The Categories page provides several ways to track your spending by category:

    1. The "Current Spend" section shows your spending against budget for each category in the current period.

    2. The "Top Categories" section ranks your most-used categories by spending amount or transaction count.

    3. For more detailed analysis, go to the Summaries page where you can generate reports showing category spending across custom date ranges.

  • Will I get reminders before recurring payments?

    Yes! By default, you'll receive notifications 2 days before any scheduled recurring payment. You can customize these notification settings in your Account Preferences. These reminders help ensure you always have sufficient funds in your account for upcoming payments.

  • How do I see a history of past recurring payments?

    All processed recurring payments appear in your regular transaction history with a special "Recurring" label. You can filter your transactions to show only recurring payments by selecting the "Recurring" filter on the Transactions page.

    Additionally, on the Recurring Purchases page, each recurring payment item has a "View History" option that shows all past instances of that specific payment.
